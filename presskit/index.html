About the Game

The Rhythm of Survival

In a world consumed by shadow, your music is your only conduit. The Shaman's Ark is a dark Metroidvania inspired by the raw aesthetics of nomadic Asian cultures. Here, silence is your enemy — and the drumbeat is your lifeblood.

Key Features

  • Ritualistic Combat — Master a unique rhythmic combat system. Your drum is a conduit for ancient power — time your strikes to the pulse of the ritual to crush the spawn of the Abyss.
  • A Nomad's Post-Apocalypse — Explore the atmospheric reaches of the Ark. From desolate steppes to decaying sanctuaries, immerse yourself in a handcrafted world where folklore meets the end of days.
  • Forbidden Power — Collect sacred amulets to build your shamanic might. Each one unlocks forbidden abilities — opening new paths and unleashing devastating echoes of the past.
  • Colossal Trials — Face gargantuan titans that demand perfect timing and an unbreakable spirit.
  • Echoes of the Past — Encounter a cast of enigmatic survivors — fallen warriors, twisted mystics — each bound to the Ark by secrets and debts older than memory.
  • The Cycle Continues — Unlock New Game+ to face a fractured, more punishing version of the world with every sacred relic intact.

The Shaman Returns

Eons ago, when the world was swallowed by the Abyss, the Great Shaman struck a Covenant with the dark and sealed a refuge for his people: The Ark — a world held aloft above the void by the Abyss's own power. But legends fade, and the darkness does not forget.

The Abyss has begun to bleed into the Ark once more, drop by black drop. Just as the last spark of hope flickers out, a figure emerges from the gloom. The Shaman has returned.

The Great Silence waits. Your drum does not.
